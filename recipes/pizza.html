Pizza

Description

Just another pizza recipe

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Mozarella
  • Tomato sauce
  • Dried italian herbs

Steps

  1. Mix together 250g of flour, 170g of water, half teaspoon of salt, teaspoon of yeast, tablespoon of olive oil
  2. Kneed for 5 minutes and leave for 30 minutes in warm place
  3. Roll dough into a pizza base"
  4. Put tomato sauce, pinch of herbs and pieces of mozarella on your pizza base"
  5. Bake in the oven at 180*C for 20min
  6. Enjoy your pizza!
